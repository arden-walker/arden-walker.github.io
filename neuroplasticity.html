Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people:

- Understanding neuroplasticity benefits people by helping them become aware of how they react to certain situations. Once we understand our reactions, we can begin to change our behaviour, which in turn allows the brain to form new or improved neural pathways. This leads to more stable cognitive function and enhances our ability to learn and is done by engaging neuroplastic mechanisms through learning, repetition and therapy. Essentially, neuroplasticity is the brains ability to rewire itself in response to stimulation and learning. In simpler terms, after an accident, when overcoming addiction or simply learning something new, the brain can find new pathways to work around changed or ineffective parts of the brain.
